Captcha and honeypot

Do not use captchas ever. Use honeypots instead.

Honeypots

Honeypots are hidden form fields that trick spam robots into entering information. If such a field contains a value, the form can be rejected.

UX Checklist

  • Honeypot fields should still have a clear label (e.g. "Do not fill this field if you are human") due to accessibility reasons
